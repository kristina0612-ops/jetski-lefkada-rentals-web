— Founded in 2026 · Rooted in Lefkada

Lefkada,
and far beyond.

Born on the Ionian, Dream Escapes is your hyper-local gateway to Lefkada and the surrounding islands — from the secluded coves of Porto Katsiki and the windswept beauty of Vassiliki Beach, to truffle hunts in Karia and quiet hours at the Faneromeni Monastery.

Modern luxury — yes. But with intention: wellness, restoration, and meaningful experiences. Including private yacht charters to Skorpios and Meganisi.
